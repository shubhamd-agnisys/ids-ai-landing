Natural-language intent becomes checked silicon handoffs.

The user does not need to think in tool order. They describe the design intent, constraints, or requested output; IDS-AI grounds that request in the golden specification, runs the right generators, checks drift, and closes CSR sign-off.

Natural language, data sheets, legacy headers, or notes describe what the user wants changed or generated.

IDS-AI turns that request into structured, checked, versioned specification intent.

Correct generators produce RTL IP, RTL SoC, UVM/RAL, headers, IP-XACT, docs, and validation assets.

The agent compares generated outputs against the source, resolves drift, and reruns the affected handoffs.

End with synchronized artifacts and 100% CSR verification coverage tied to the final specification.
